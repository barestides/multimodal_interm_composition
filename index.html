Arcane Survival

Arcane Survival is a minecraft server centered around semi-vanilla, 100% legitimate gameplay. It has existed for about two and a half years and has had thousands of players join.

Communication on Arcane

There are three main methods for communication on Arcane:

Forums

 Forums are where more persistent discussion takes place. Posts are indexed and easily searchable.

Discord

 Discord is the most popular form of communication offered. It provides irc-like text chatting as well as support for voice channels.

In Game Chat

Effectively Communicating And Resolving Disputes

Communicating in an online environment can be difficult at times. Not being able to physically see or hear the other members of a conversation means one has to be more careful when sending messages via text. Discord provides Arcane with voice chat functionality, which can help alleviate the majority of these issues. Whenever possible, if you have a dispute with someone, you should take it to voice chat and talk it out, rather than smash you keyboard hoping your words reach the other person in the way you intended.
